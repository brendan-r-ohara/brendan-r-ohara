About Me

My Journey

Growing up in the Southwest, between the vast landscapes of New Mexico and Colorado, I developed a deep appreciation for the complexities of environmental issues. My upbringing on a farm taught me the importance of water rights, while witnessing my parents' dedication to caring for Navajo uranium miners instilled in me a sense of compassion and responsibility. I witnessed firsthand the ebb and flow of communities dependent on industries like oil and gas.

My journey took a pivotal turn when I relocated to the US Virgin Islands, where I discovered the wonders of coral reefs and the urgent need to protect them. This experience ignited a passion for environmental conservation and advocacy. Upon moving to Florida, I pursued my degrees, combining my diverse experiences to focus on leveraging science-backed policies to address the pressing challenges of climate change and the injustices it perpetuates. My goal is to enact positive change by tackling the climate crisis head-on and advocating for policies that promote environmental sustainability and social equity.
