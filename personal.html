About Me

Background

I'm Brendan O'Hara, an Environmental Science student at the University of South Florida. Growing up in the Southwest and later moving to the US Virgin Islands, I developed a passion for environmental issues. These experiences fueled my desire to make a positive impact through evidence-based policy and environmental advocacy.

Interests

In my free time, I enjoy scuba diving, hiking, and photography. These activities allow me to stay connected to nature and inspire my work in environmental science and policy. I also enjoy technology and coding, often working on personal projects that reflect my commitment to sustainability.

Let's Connect

I'm always open to networking and connecting with others in the field of environmental science. You can reach me via email or LinkedIn for professional discussions and collaborations.
